Sobre Move and Shipping

At Move and Shipping, we understand that an international move is much more than transporting belongings from one country to another — it marks the beginning of a new stage in life. That is why we combine experience, planning and humanized service to offer complete, safe and personalized solutions for families and companies around the world.

Founded by specialists with extensive experience in logistics and international moving, our mission is to provide a smooth, organized and transparent experience at every stage of the process. From the first contact to final delivery, we work with commitment, efficiency and attention to detail.

We have a global network of strategic partners and a prepared team to offer complete support in packing, sea and air transportation, documentation, customs clearance, storage and personalized follow-up. More than moving goods, we connect people to their new destinations with trust, dedication and quality.
