FAQ

What documents are required for moving to Brazil?

The required documentation varies depending on your profile and the type of relocation. Our team will guide you through every step.

Can I ship used furniture and personal belongings to Brazil?

Yes, provided that the applicable regulations for unaccompanied household goods and the required documentation are met.

Do you assist with customs clearance in Brazil?

Yes. Depending on the services contracted, we can guide and assist you throughout the customs clearance process.

How long does it take to relocate to Brazil?

The transit time depends on the country of origin, shipping method, documentation requirements, and customs clearance.
