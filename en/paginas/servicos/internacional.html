FAQ

Which option is better: ocean freight or air freight?

Air freight is generally recommended for smaller shipments and shorter transit times. Ocean freight is usually the most suitable option for larger relocations.

Do you assist with the required documentation?

Yes. Our team will guide you through all the documentation required based on the origin, destination, and type of relocation.

Can I purchase moving insurance?

Yes. We can help you choose the best insurance option to protect your belongings during transportation.

Can my belongings be stored before delivery?

Yes. Depending on your needs, we offer secure and flexible storage solutions.
